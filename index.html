MAYHEM

A BatCave in Soho is a tabletop RPG set in Soho, an area in the city of Westminster, London in 1982.

You are a vampire in a band that regularly plays at The Batcave, an extremely popular club and birthplace of goth culture that has seen the likes of Bauhaus, Souxsie and the Banshees, and The Cure. There is a small community of vampires in Soho, and most of them stalk The Batcave nightly in search of a good time, or their next meal.

We hope you enjoy your stay.
